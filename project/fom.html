Introduction: Meaning of Management

  • Businesses need inputs (money, materials, machines, men and processes) to convert into products or services as output.


  • With the same inputs the same output may not be achieved every time and various activities need proper direction, coordination and integration.


  • The process of using various resources (inputs) to produce results (outputs) is known as management. The degree of success varies according to efficiency.


  • Thus, management refers to the process of using men, money, machines, material and processes through proper direction, coordination and integration of several activities to produce desired results.

Definition of Management

F.W. Taylor, ‘Management is an art of knowing what to do when to do and seeing that it is done in the best and cheapest way’.
Harold Koontz, ‘Management is an art of getting things done through and with the people in formally organised groups.
