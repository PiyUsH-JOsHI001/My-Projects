Management Vs Administration

Major differences between management and administration are:

  1. Meaning: Management is a systematic way of managing people and things within the organisation. The administration is defined as an act of administering the whole organisation by a group of people.
  2. Level: Management is an activity of business and functional level, whereas Administration is a high-level activity.
  3. Focus: Management focuses on policy implementation, policy formulation is performed by the administration.
  4. Functions: of administration include legislation and determination. Functions of management are executive and governing.
  5. Type of decision: Administration takes all the important decisions of the organisation while management makes decisions under the boundaries set by the administration.
  6. People: A group of persons, who are employees of the organisation, is collectively known as management. On the other hand, administration represents the owners of the organisation.
  7. Type of Organisation: Management can be seen in the profit making organisation like business enterprises. Conversely, the Administration is found in government and military offices, clubs, hospitals, religious organisations and all the non-profit making enterprises
